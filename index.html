Help
Critical Clicks
When clicking the Winnie Orb, you have a 1 out of 100 chance of getting a critical click. Critical clicks give you anywhere from 1.5 to 3 times your WPC. Upgrades and items can help increase the chance of getting a critical click and increase the value of getting critical clicks. You can also view your critical click chance in the statistics menu; the lower, the better.
Your WPC has a multiplier added to it based on how many times you have gotten a critical click. Every time you get a critical click the multiplier gets added by 0.001. It doesn't sound like much, but when you get a 20/10 Vision Golden Winnie it can make a big difference.
Prestige
When you reach 1 million Winnie Orbs, you gain 1 prestige token. A prestige token gives a +10% increase to your WPS and WPC. Everytime you get a a token, the goal to get another one increases by x1.1, meaning if you just got a token for reaching 1 million total Winnie Orbs, you would have to get another 1.1 million Winnie orbs for your next token.
When you prestige you lose all Winnie Orbs, items, tools, and upgrades, but claims your prestige tokens which boosts your WPS and WPC based on how many you got. After prestiging, your goal to get another prestige token resets back to 1 million, meaning that you can exponentially more prestige tokens the more you play.
